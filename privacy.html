Privacy

Simple privacy practices for a simple consulting site.

Cadence Health Media collects only the information submitted through website forms or booking links so we can respond to inquiries, send requested resources, and prepare for strategy conversations.

What is collected

Name, work email, company, selected consulting need, and any message you choose to submit. Please do not submit patient health information, confidential campaign data, or sensitive personal information through the website.

How it is used

Information is used to respond to your request, send the planning resource, schedule calls, and understand whether Cadence Health Media can help with your media planning needs.

Forms

Form handling depends on the hosting or CRM tool connected at launch. The site is prepared for static form collection.

Analytics

The site includes event hooks for CTA and form tracking. No analytics platform sends data until a tracking container is added.

Contact

Questions about website inquiry data can be sent to hello@cadencehealthmedia.com.
