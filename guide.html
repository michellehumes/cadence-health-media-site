The HCP Media Planning Diagnostic

A compact, decision-oriented checklist for healthcare and pharma teams that want to identify weak spots before a campaign, annual plan, or partner recommendation moves forward.

What the diagnostic covers

  • Audience clarity and segmentation readiness
  • Channel roles across paid, owned, CRM, and field touchpoints
  • Measurement design, KPI hierarchy, and reporting cadence
  • Vendor responsibilities, handoffs, and governance risks
  • Conversion paths for inquiries, demos, content downloads, and bookings

You will land on a page with the printable diagnostic and a strategy-call option.

Inside The Diagnostic

A planning tool, not a generic checklist.

The resource scores six areas that usually decide whether HCP media plans become executable: audience clarity, channel roles, content readiness, measurement design, partner accountability, and conversion path quality.

Use it before annual planning

Spot strategic gaps while there is still room to adjust scope, budget, measurement, and partner expectations.

Use it before an RFP

Clarify what partners should solve so recommendations can be compared against the same decision criteria.

Use it before launch

Confirm that optimization signals, ownership, and reporting routines are in place before performance questions arrive.
