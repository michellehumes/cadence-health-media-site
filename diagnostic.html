Decision Questions

  • Which HCP segment must the plan win first, and why?
  • What job does each channel perform that another channel cannot?
  • Where will audience quality be verified before optimization decisions are made?
  • Which partner owns measurement interpretation, not just report delivery?
  • What would trigger a budget shift, channel pause, content change, or audience refinement?
  • What is the single most important next action for a qualified prospect or stakeholder?

Interpretation

6-12: The plan likely needs strategic repair before partner execution.

13-18: The plan has useful pieces, but governance and measurement may create execution drag.

19-24: The plan is close to launch-ready. Focus on optimization cadence and stakeholder reporting.
