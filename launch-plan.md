# Cadence Health Media CEO Launch Plan

## Launch Position

Cadence Health Media should be positioned as senior operator support for healthcare and pharma teams that need HCP media plans to become clearer, more measurable, and easier for partners to execute.

The site now sells:

- HCP media strategy
- Omnichannel planning
- Analytics and measurement
- Vendor and partner orchestration
- Lead generation and Calendly conversion

It does not rely on past employer names, logo borrowing, public dollar claims, or unsupported performance claims.

## Launch Sequence

1. Confirm the final domain points to the hosting provider.
2. Confirm the inbox `hello@cadencehealthmedia.com` exists or replace it globally.
3. Confirm the Calendly URL `https://calendly.com/cadencehealthmedia/strategy-call` exists or replace it globally.
4. Deploy the folder to Netlify or another static host.
5. Submit `https://cadencehealthmedia.com/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
6. Add a lightweight analytics container only after deciding whether cookie notice language is needed.
7. Send the homepage, services page, and diagnostic page to three trusted healthcare media operators for positioning feedback.

## Conversion Priorities

- Primary conversion: strategy call.
- Secondary conversion: diagnostic download.
- Tertiary conversion: qualified inquiry form.

The best first optimization target is Calendly click-through from the homepage and contact page. The second is diagnostic form completion.

## First 30 Days

- Publish one LinkedIn post per week around a specific planning problem.
- Use the diagnostic as the recurring lead magnet.
- Add one anonymized insight article after the first few discovery calls.
- Review form submissions and Calendly conversion weekly.
- Refine the services page based on the words prospects use on calls.

## Analytics Events Already Instrumented

- `book_strategy_call_click`
- `planning_guide_click`
- `calendly_click`
- `thank_you_calendly_click`
- `diagnostic_download_click`
- `diagnostic_calendly_click`
- `form_submit`

## CEO Standard Before Launch

- No employer-name borrowing.
- No dollar claims.
- No patient health information requested.
- Clear call booking path.
- Clear diagnostic lead magnet.
- Repeatable QA command passes.
- Local browser flow has been tested.
